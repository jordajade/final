Who We Are


Get ready for an amazing basketball experience with Little Hoopers! Founded in 2019 in Sacramento, CA, we are a non-profit organization dedicated to our community. Our goal is to empower young athletes through an array of exciting programs, such as action-packed summer camps, competitive basketball teams, thrilling leagues, and adrenaline-pumping tournaments. We are thrilled to provide top-notch basketball activities in a safe environment for boys and girls aged 5-18 from all walks of life. Get ready to join the fun!

What We Do


Join us for an incredible basketball experience at Little Hoopers! Our programs are tailored to meet your unique needs. Whether you're a beginner looking to start your basketball journey or a seasoned player seeking a challenge, we've got you covered. In the summer, join our comprehensive skills program to take your abilities to the next level.Little Hoopers guarantees an exciting basketball journey!
